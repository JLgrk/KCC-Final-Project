Stop! Before you recycle, consider: how can you reuse your metal products?

  • Punch holes in the bottom of aluminum cans and use as planters.
  • Decorate your cans and use them as desktop pencil holders.
  • Use smaller cans in drawers to organize small items.
  • Cut a design into the sides of a can and place a candle inside.

How to Recycle Metal Items

Metal cans, lids, foil wrap and foil trays can all be recycled. Items must be clean of any food scraps and dry. Aluminum foil should be crumpled into a ball.

Do NOT place scrap metal, pipes, tools, fencing, electronics or knifes into metal recyling. When in doubt, ask, don't be a wishful recycler!
