Stop! Before Recycling...

Have you considered how you might be able to reuse or upcycle that cardboard?

  • Fold up old boxes and store for later moves, gifts, mailing, or storage.
  • Compost it! Add clean, un-dyed cardboard do your compost pile and it will breakdown naturally.
  • Make mulch! Shredding clean cardboard can make a great insulator for plants.
  • Get crafty. There are tons of ways to upcycle old boxes into works of art.
  • Cat toys. Nothing makes a cat happier than a box to hide in.

How to Recycle Cardboard

Recycling cardboard is easy but there are some tricky things to navigate. Cardboard that's stained with food cannot be recycled - that means no recycling pizza boxes or takeout containers. When you recycle boxes, make sure to remove all tape and labels. Fold and compact your cardboard and pop it into the bin for cardboard and paper.
