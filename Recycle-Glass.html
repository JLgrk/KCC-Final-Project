What happens when you recycle your glass?

So many homes contain ample amounts of glass on a yearly basis. Take the time to look at where this glass could go, and where it should go. If recycled properly the glass in homes that is no longer in use will be sent to be processed, washed, and crushed, and ready to be reheated to make more glass product. It's super easy to take a moment and put the glass used in homes back into curculation. Just a quick rinse with some water and then head to the nearest recycling deposit to drop off.
