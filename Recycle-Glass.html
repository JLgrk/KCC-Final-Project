Stop. Before you recycle. How can you reuse your glass products?

  • Reusable Water Bottle.
  • Wine Bottle Feeder. Upside down your glass bottle goes, poke some big holes in the top with a plate under it and you got a bird feeder.
  • Glass Bottle Liquid Soap Dispencer. Just buy the soap despencer top and grab your glass bottle.
  • Upcycled Lamp. Create the top of the bottle into the bottom of a lamp.
  • Sand Art Crafts. If you have colored sand, than you will have beachy decor.
  • Glass Planter. Plants love to live in glass. The glass lets all the light in.

How to recycle your Glass

So many homes contain ample amounts of glass on a yearly basis. Take the time to look at where this glass could go, and where it should go. If recycled properly the glass in homes that is no longer in use will be sent to be processed, washed, and crushed, and ready to be reheated to make more glass product. It's super easy to take a moment and put the glass used in homes back into curculation. Just a quick rinse with some water and then head to the nearest recycling deposit to drop off.
